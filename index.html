Drupal platforms the public actually depends on.

01 / 03

Higher Education · Research

UCSF Department of Anesthesia

Challenge
An aging Drupal platform and complex content made it hard to manage profiles, publications, and accessibility while keeping the site up to date.
Y3TI approach
Led migrations from Drupal 5 to 7 and 7 to 10, built custom modules for profile and PubMed integration, and introduced a flexible, component-based structure with accessibility built in.
Outcome
A modern, scalable Drupal site with 99% WCAG 2.1 accessibility, streamlined content management, and a strong foundation for long-term growth.
Visit the Department of Anesthesia.
02 / 03

Higher Education · Admissions

California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo

Challenge
Multiple sites across separate codebases made updates, consistency, and authentication difficult to manage.
Y3TI approach
Built a Drupal 10 upstream on Pantheon with a shared codebase across 10+ sites, split config for flexibility, plus SSO and custom editor tools.
Outcome
A scalable multi-site platform with shared updates, improved consistency, and easier site management.
Visit Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.
03 / 03

Government · Civic Services

The City of Berkeley

Challenge
Outdated systems made it difficult to manage public services, handle 311 requests, and communicate quickly during urgent situations.
Y3TI approach
Upgraded the platform to Drupal 10, adding SSO, custom editor tools, 311 form integration, and emergency alert capabilities to support real-time needs.
Outcome
A more responsive city website that supports public services, improves communication during emergencies, and makes updates easier for staff.
Visit the City of Berkeley.
04 / 04

Higher Education · Headless Drupal Development

UCSF Campus Life Services

Challenge
Campus Life Services needed a fast, accessible site that could support multiple services and integrate with existing systems.
Y3TI approach
Built a decoupled Drupal 10 site with Nuxt.js, integrating Salesforce Agent and SSO, with accessibility built in from the start.
Outcome
A high-performance, fully accessible platform that connects services, streamlines access, and improves the overall campus experience.
Visit UCSF Campus Life Services
